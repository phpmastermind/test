Replying to a message is just as easy as composing a new one. In the following tutorial we will show you how to open a message and send a reply.

Replying to a Message

  • First you must login
  • Click on 'Messages' at the top of the page
  • Choose the message you want to view
  • Click 'Reply' to the right of the message subject
  • When replying to messages the 'Subject' field is already filled in so all you need to do is enter your response 'Message'
  • Finally click 'Send'
