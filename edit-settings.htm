When installing Mokoala you choose the settings for your site such as the admin email and the site's name. If you made any mistakes or want to change any of these settings then follow one of the guides below.

Video of Editing Settings

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1

Place your cursor over the 'Dashboard' link in the header and select 'Settings'.

Step 2

Make the changes that you need. and click 'Save Changes'.

Step 3

Upon the changes being saved correctly you will recieve a confirmation message.
